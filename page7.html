A Career Conversation campaign is running globally. Why is it important to you to discuss your career with your manager by August 31st?

In an era of a constant rapid changing environment, driven through technology innovation like AI and automation, the need to upskill and reskill the workforce has never been so important. Taking ownership of your own career plan is pivotal to build the right skills that will enable your career. Your career plan can be build based on the following questions:

1) Where do you see your career going? Think short and very long term!

2) What skills do you need? IBM is transforming quickly and by 2030, 10% of the available jobs will be ones we have even not imagined yet.

3) What help do you need?

in this journey you are in the driving seat, but you are not alone! You can always seek support:


1) Your manager provides clarity on your career path, next positions and hot roles

2) You get advice to keep your skills relevant: learning, Leadership development, digital credentials, stretched projects, mentoring or shadowing

3) Improve through personal tips, experience and best practices from your manager

4) You take full control on your career.

5) You learn about new career tracks and internal job movement that you never thought before.

6) Invest in your yourself, in your own personal development
