MEA Stellar is a program designed to accelerate the professional growth of high potential B 8-9 employees by developing their leadership skills through a customized and tailored learning plan.  For their final delivery to the program, the Stellar inaugural class of 32 talents came together for a 2-day workshop in Dubai.

Takreem El Tohamy, GM IBM MEA, opened the session and shared his insights on IBM strategy to influence the market and essential behaviors of great and successful leaders. He conversed with the participants on people strategy and business imperatives through examples of his personal career.

The first day was dedicated to business projects presentations. The teams have invested 8 weeks on big idea / business challenge. They built the business case from a concept to a potential workable solution.

A panel of MEA executive leaders: Maged Wassim, Miguel Vega, Defne Tozan, Craig Holmes, Andy Parkinson, Ebi Emafo and Hossam Seif El-Din, evaluated each team presentation and gave constructive feedback. Some of the projects got strong interest and have been sponsored for further development. 

The overarching outcome is clearly the key experiential learning opportunity for Stellars to tackle areas of strategic importance to the business.

Day 2 was mainly spent in Business presentations (Blockchain, Large deals, HR imperatives) as well as specific coaching circles designed to develop the following themes: Resilience, Storytelling, Negotiation, Leadership purpose & Leading other

Overall, the workshop provided interesting and relevant insight into leadership and exceptional networking with executives.  The participants walked away with a better understanding of strategic priorities, and a stronger sense of how they can personally influence the business and transformation in their teams.
