"The #HappyIBMer campaign, inspired from the UAE Happiness Index was born out of a desire to reinforce a people oriented culture, to improve engagement among IBMers and to create a sense of pride, community and fun at work.

It's always been my passion to engage IBMers beyond their regular work scope. I get immense satisfaction in putting together events that leave a positive impact on all involved and enhances their job satisfaction. To that end, I created and executed numerous activities over the past six months such as the Colleague Appreciation Days, UAE Children’s Day, the IBMer International Potluck Lunch, and the monthly birthday celebrations which brought together employees and their families. The feedback received from IBMers was extremely positive with high NPS scores across all activities held.

This month, we will be hosting IBM Summer camps for children of IBMers who wish to learn more about IBM Cloud, machine learning and building simple chatbots. After all, it’s a very simple formula: happy employees = happy customers."

Nathalie Chamas
